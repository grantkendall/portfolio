Improvised Landscapes, 2024

A study to explore the layered, seamless spaces of digital space in a printed format. Vellum paper was used to add another element of composition, namely layering textures. At the same time, black from an inkjet printer was the only color used, flattening the dimensionality of 3D images.

Dissent/Descent, 2017

Part protest piece, part linguistic exploration. My first explorations with conceptual artworks began in the space of puzzles. Standing in front of the work, the viewer's head is hidden in the black void, creating a temporary lack of identity.

Perennial Institute Ideation, 2018

Design mock-ups, sketches and ideas for a the Perennial Institute, a summer retreat in Berlin.

A Visual Representation of Letter Usage, 2017

Typography as map... Linguistics as statistics... A book as a pure container for information. Vellum, spiral-bound book with hand-cut circles that represent the frequency of letter usage in the English language. Currently resides at the MCAD library under the ISBN number XXXXXX. Won the first prize in the 2017 MCAD Student Artist's Book Competition.
